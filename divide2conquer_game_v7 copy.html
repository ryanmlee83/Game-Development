📖 Rules of Play

Divide II Conquer — Meiosis Card Game

Objective

Be the first player to complete all 8 phases of meiosis in order by playing Phase Cards and answering Challenge Questions. The final phase — Telophase II & Cytokinesis — requires a harder Violet question to win.

Turn Structure

  • Draw: Draw 2 cards from the main deck to start your turn.
  • Play: Play up to 3 cards (3 actions). Each play costs 1 action.
  • End Turn: Discard down to 5 cards if needed, then pass to the next player.

Card Types

🔮 Phase Card 🏆 Final Phase ☣️ Mutation ⚡ Action 🛡️ Intervention 🌀 Wild Phase 🔭 DNA Probe
  • Phase Cards — Play the next phase in your sequence. Answer a 🟢 Green question to advance. Must be played in order (Prophase I → … → Telophase II).
  • Final Phase — Telophase II & Cytokinesis requires a 🟣 Violet question to win.
  • Mutation Cards — Target an opponent. Answer a Green question; if correct, the mutation takes effect on them (up to 2 active at once).
  • Action Cards — Target an opponent. They may block by answering a Violet question. Unblocked actions set back or skip the target.
  • Intervention Cards — No question needed. Either place as a Shield on yourself (auto-blocks the matching incoming mutation) or cure a target's active mutation.
  • Wild Phase Cards 🌀⭐ — Substitutes for any phase in a set range. Wild: Meiosis I covers phases 1–4, Wild: Meiosis II covers phases 5–7, Wild Phase (universal) covers any non-final phase. Still requires a Green Challenge.
  • DNA Probe 🔭 — No question needed. Look at the top 5 cards of the deck and take one into your hand. The rest return to the top of the deck. Costs 1 action.

Mutations & Shields

  • Each player can hold up to 2 active mutations and 2 active shields.
  • Point Mutation — Must answer an extra Green Challenge per phase.
  • Frameshift — Skip every other turn until cured.
  • Chromosomal Deletion — Immediately discard 2 cards.
  • Translocation — Swap hands with another player.
  • Shields auto-consume when a matching mutation is played against you.

Special Rules

  • Anaphase I — If you answer incorrectly, you must retry this phase next turn (the card is discarded but you keep your progress).
  • Exchange Hand — Discard your entire hand (min. 5 cards) and draw 5 new ones. Must be your only action and ends your turn immediately.
  • Spindle Malfunction — Prevents playing Metaphase I or II for one turn. The card stays in your hand.
  • Challenge! Action — Target loses their next turn.

Challenge Questions

  • 🟢 Green (DOK 1–2) — Used for Phase Cards and Mutation Cards.
  • 🟣 Violet (DOK 3–4) — Used to block Action Cards and to win the final phase.
  • If a timer is enabled, you have 60 seconds to select and confirm your answer. Time-out counts as an incorrect answer.
