How to Play

A quick guide to the cell cycle
🎯 OBJECTIVE
Be the first player to reach Cytokinesis by correctly answering biology questions as you race through the cell cycle.
📋 ON YOUR TURN
🟢 GREEN QUESTION
Basic recall. Answer correctly → move 1 space.
🟣 VIOLET QUESTION
Application & analysis. Answer correctly → move 2 spaces.
⬦ SPECIAL SPACES
💠
CHECKPOINTS — Three checkpoint activities: DNA base-pair matching (G1), True/False rapid fire (G2), and vocabulary matching (M). Each has a 30-second timer. Pass → advance 1 space.
🃏
CELL EVENTS — Draw a card. Could boost you forward, grant immunity, or set you back. Such is biology.
💤
G0 PHASE — The resting state. All players start here before entering the active cycle at G1.
⚗ STUDY MODE
Toggle Relaxed Mode on the setup screen to disable all checkpoint timers — checkpoints still require correct answers, but there's no time pressure. Great for classrooms learning the material for the first time.
